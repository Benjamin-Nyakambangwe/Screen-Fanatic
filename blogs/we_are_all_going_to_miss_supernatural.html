WE ARE ALL GOING TO MISS SUPERNATURAL

After announcing the end of popular DC Comics television series Arrow just earlier this month, The CW brought an end to another fan-favorite show, as the adventures of the Winchester brothers on Supernatural will come to a close after its 15th season. Stars Jared Padalecki, Jensen Ackles, and Misha Collins all took to social media on Friday to make the announcement.

The series began in 2005, back when The CW network was still known as The WB, and has become the longest-running American sci-fi/fantasy series ever with over 300 episodes under its belt and a dedicated fanbase to match.
